- flagset = `{flags}`
- list flags of a given type via: `flag {flag_type}` 
- flag types:
  - `?`: question
  - `*`: brainstorm
  - `!` = important
  ~ `*` = in progress (display visually in indexes)
  ~ `+` = include in-file markers in the outline (when used within an outline)
  ~ `d=YYYYMMDD` = date flag
~ `f` snippet: inserts a flag (`{$1}`)
